Homebase

Description

New design of Homebase e-commerce website with a clean flat look. Main targets were ease of use, browser compatibility and accessibility.

My role was to develop the front-end, based on a provided design guide - both for desktop and mobile versions of the site. This included development of views and modules that were interfaced with a backend solution running on WebSphere Commerce. Work was done in a small agile team and the implementation was consulted with the backend team.

Client

Homebase is a British home improvement store and garden centre, with 342 stores across the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland.
